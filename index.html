About Us

About Us

The TRAA Works at a Grassroots Level Within the Thames River Watershed to . . .

  • Protect, enhance and regenerate a viable, multi-species, year-round fishery.
  • Promote and participate in environmental clean-up, pollution control and rehabilitation.
  • Breed, relocate and stock to re-establish or augment appropriate species of fish.
  • Promote environmentally sound sport fishing practices.
  • Form partnerships with community businesses, government agencies, special interest groups, landowners, and other stakeholders in the Thames River watershed.
  • Increase public awareness of the unique diversity of the Thames River Watershed, its challenges, its recreational opportunities and its vital importance to our community

Over the past couple of years the folks at Melchers Construction Ltd have offered up their beautiful boardroom for TRAA executive meetings. It's an ideal space with all the amenities required for us to accomplish all the “behind the scenes work.” Many thanks go out to the continued support of Melchers Construction Ltd.

Before we could take the trailer to Try Recycling, we needed to get the spare tire for the trailer fixed. Pat Devincenzo, proprietor of Angling Sports, offered to get it repaired. The tire needed replacing and Pat took care of it. TRAA members recieve a discount equal to the tax on anthything in the store. Thanks Pat, your generosity is greatly appreciated.

The TRAA voted unanimously to re-name the TRAA trout hatchery honouring Keith Wales' memory and his unselfish support of the TRAA's work in the London area, in particular Komoka Creek. Special thanks go out to Muma Manufacturing in St. Thomas for donating the beautiful laser-cut aluminum sign.

Members of the trout hatchery rotation know the woes we've had with lighting in the hatchery. Even with larger fixtures installed a few years ago, it was still frustrating on days when the mercury dipped below -15C and the fluorescent technology teased us with its dull flicker. Enter Mike Jeffries of Guillevin Greentech in London with nice set of bright and dependable LED fixtures.
